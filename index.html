Who We Are

A Wake-Up Call for Self-Renewal

Since 1999, concerned people have noticed that the Northern part of Nigeria has been on a drift towards a systematic and unabated decline. The Northern Reform Organization emerged as a collective effort to address escalating problems and challenges of Northern Nigeria.

We aim to rescue this important part of the country, once proudly considered the back bone of Nigeria's economic, social and political life, from failing and falling into the trap of disintegration.

Our History & Mission

About the Organization

Credit for the establishment of Northern Reform Organization (NRO) is attributable to a category of concerned elders that include retired Technocrats, Captains of Industries, Businessmen as well as Enterprising Individuals.

The Organization was officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission on the 5th of December, 2019. Under the able guidance of our leadership, we have mobilized around the idea of rejuvenating the Northern Region concept.

Our Vision

To bring about Moral Re-birth, Social Justice and Political Unity to the Northern Region.

Our Mission

To restore harmony, sustain peace and economic development within the Region. We aim to enthrone a viable identity value system for the all-round good of the Region.

Implementation Plans

Strategic Initiatives

State Development Plans

Spearhead the design of Development Plan, with focus on key areas, namely: Security, Education, Agriculture, Small and Medium Scale Enterprises (SMEs), Infrastructure and Commerce.

Cooperative Financing

Establish and register a Cooperative Financing Agency (CFA), to finance the proposed Development Plans to reposition agricultural production, commercial and industrial activities in the North.

Northern Farmers Association

Establish a Coordinating Authority for the development of a powerful Northern Farmers Association that brings together different associations like RIFAN, Wheat producers, Potato farmers and others.

Power & Infrastructure

Encourage the establishment of Public and Independent Power Plants in the North to drive sustainable industrial growth and infrastructure development.

Waste Management Industry

Encourage the establishment of Garbage and Waste matter processing industries by each State Government to process recycled materials and produce Organic Fertilizer and Bio-gas.

Strategic Communication

Develop galvanizing Television and Radio programs that would be self-financing to redirect, refocus, reinforce and empower the people.
